== Description ==

Awesome Google Adsense is a free plugin that automatically insert Google Adsense ads into your Posts, Pages and Sidebar. You'll never need to copy and paste codes again, let Awesome Google Adsense plugin do the hard job for you.

It's fully compatible with widgets, multi-lingual wordpress standards and iPad themes.

You say when, where and how you want to show your Adsense ads and Awesome Google Adsense do it. It's that simple.

We encourage you to give a try to Awesome Google Adsense.

Don't forget to pay us a beer, set up the donation settings.

**Important**
As stated in Google Adsense program policies. AdSense publishers are not permitted to place Google ads on pages with pornographic content.
This includes photos or videos which contain full nudity or sexual activities. More information about this policy can be found in Adsense help center (https://www.google.com/adsense/support/as/bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=105957).

== Installation ==

1. Upload the folder `awesome-google-adsense 'to folder ` / wp-content/plugins / `
2. Activate the plugin Awesome Google Adsense through the 'Plugins' menu in the administration page of your WordPress
3. Configure the plugin through the menu 'Settings'

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need to copy and paste codes? =

No, you don't need to copy and paste codes to show your Google Adsense ads.

= Is it hard to setup the Awesome Google Adsense plugin? =

No, it's very simple to set it up. Just a few seconds and it shows your Adsense ads like magic.

= Can I choose the type and placement of ad unit to be displayed? =

Yes, you customize easily to match your blog's look and feel.

= Does it complies with Google Adsense policies? =

Yes, it complies with Google Adsense policies.

= Can I use  this plugin at adult/pornography sites? =

No, you can't use this plugin to show ads at adult/pornography sites. As stated in Google Adsense program policies. AdSense publishers are not permitted to place Google ads on pages with pornographic content.
This includes photos or videos which contain full nudity or sexual activities. More information about this policy can be found in Adsense help center (https://www.google.com/adsense/support/as/bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=105957).
